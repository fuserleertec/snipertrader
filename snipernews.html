The Institutional Paradigm: Avoid chasing market orders into an empty depth of market (DOM). High-impact events wipe out top-of-book liquidity instantaneously. Instead of a naive "stop-entry straddle" which triggers toxic adverse selection slippage, institutional models deploy passive multi-layered limit price arrays inside the anticipated liquidity vacuum.
