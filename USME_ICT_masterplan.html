🎯 The USME v3.1 Setup Filter — Mechanical Entry Criteria

Every trade must pass all four filters before execution. No exceptions. This is the mechanical foundation of the Masterplan.

Core Principle: "We don't predict. We react to confirmed institutional footprint."

📋 THE 4-STEP SETUP FILTER

  • ❶
    Killzone Confirmation — LONDON 2:00–5:00 AM ET or NY 8:30–11:00 AM ET. Only trade during these institutional sessions. Off-hours signals are filtered out.
  • ❷
    HTF Bias Alignment — Weekly and Daily must agree. W:BULL + D:BULL = only longs. W:BEAR + D:BEAR = only shorts. Mixed = no trade.
  • ❸
    Smart Money Score ≥ 80% — ELITE (90%+) or PRIME (80–89%) required. The score evaluates confluence: FVG, OB, CHoCH, SMT divergence, and volume profile.
  • ❹
    Closed Candle Rule — NEVER enter on a forming candle. Wait for the bar to close confirming the setup. Enter on the open of the next candle.

⚡ The USME v3.1 Execution Workflow

Once a setup passes the filter, follow this mechanical execution protocol without deviation.

Key Insight: "We execute based on tier, not emotion. The SOTD engine tells us the probability — we follow the math."

📋 THE 6-STEP EXECUTION PROTOCOL

  • ❶
    Entry Trigger — Enter on the open of the next candle after the signal bar closes. Use a limit order at the FVG/OB level for optimal fill.
  • ❷
    Initial Stop Loss — Place 1.5x ATR(14) below the entry for longs, above for shorts. This provides room for institutional noise while protecting capital.
  • ❸
    Position Sizing — Based on SOTD tier (see Risk Panel). ELITE = 0.5% risk, PRIME = 0.35%, STANDARD = 0.2%.
  • ❹
    Target 1 (T1) — 1.5x risk. Move stop loss to breakeven. Lock in protection. No trade becomes a losing trade after T1.
  • ❺
    Target 2 (T2) — 3x risk. Take 50% off the table. Let the remainder run with a trailing stop.
  • ❻
    Target 3 (T3) — 5x risk. Manage with 1.5x ATR trailing stop. Let winners run until the trailing stop is hit.

📐 Trade Management Rules
  • ❶
    Move to Breakeven — After T1 (1.5x risk), move stop to entry. Now it's a free trade.
  • ❷
    Partial Take Profit — At T2 (3x risk), close 50%. Let the rest run.
  • ❸
    Trailing Stop — After T2, activate 1.5x ATR trailing stop on the remainder.
  • ❹
    No Adding — Never add to a losing position. Never average down. Let the trade play out.
📈 The 1:3 Rule: If a trade cannot reach 1:3 risk-to-reward, it is not worth taking. Skip it.
