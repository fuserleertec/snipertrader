Why Baseline Works

The science behind self-monitoring.

01
🔬
Emotional Data as Signal
Steenbarger demonstrated that traders who log their emotional state before sessions show measurable improvement in rule-following within 30 days. The act of naming an emotion reduces its power to override your plan.
02
💓
Heart Rate and Decision Quality
Research shows decision quality degrades significantly when resting heart rate exceeds 85 BPM. Professional traders operating in high-stakes environments maintain lower baseline HR through deliberate pre-session regulation.
03
🔄
The Pre-Session Ritual Effect
A consistent pre-session ritual creates a neurological "state switch" — signaling your brain that you are entering a deliberate, rule-governed environment. This reduces impulsive limbic responses to market movement.

"The most successful traders I have worked with treat their emotional states as data, not as problems to be suppressed. They use self-monitoring not to eliminate emotion, but to channel it into sharper perception of market patterns."

— Inspired by Brett N. Steenbarger · The Psychology of Trading
