Origin Story

Failure Was The
First Blueprint

I didn't start TradeSniper.ai because I saw a market opportunity. I started it because I needed these tools to survive in the markets. Every blown account, every missed entry, every 4am session at the charts — it all demanded something better.

After years grinding through ICT's methodology — learning how Smart Money operates, how liquidity is engineered, how institutions sweep retail stops before moving price — I realized the tooling didn't match the knowledge. ThinkorSwim had no ICT-native suite. Pine Script indicators were scattered, inconsistent, and built by people who had never traded a live funded account.

So I built what I needed. USME wasn't designed. It was demanded. And every tool in this ecosystem followed the same principle: if it doesn't make a real trader more dangerous in the markets, it doesn't ship.

"The edge isn't in seeing more. It's in seeing clearly — and executing without hesitation. TradeSniper exists to give you that clarity."
