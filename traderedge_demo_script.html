How to use this script: Each scene has a VOICEOVER block (what you say) and a SCREEN block (what you do on screen). Record your screen at full resolution, then record voiceover separately or live. Recommended: Loom, QuickTime, or OBS Studio.

Keep the pace brisk. The goal is to show the app feels fast and purposeful — not to explain every feature. Let the visuals do the heavy lifting.

SCREEN

01
MODULE 01 — Pre-Session Baseline
0:08 – 0:22
SCREEN
App is on the BASELINE tab. Click FLOW — the green alert animates in: "FLOW STATE CONFIRMED." Slowly drag the Heart Rate slider up to ~105 BPM — color shifts from green to red. Drag back down to 72.
VOICE
"Before you touch a chart, you check in. Emotional state. Heart rate. Sleep. Stress. If you're in revenge mode or spiking over 100 BPM — the app tells you to walk away."
SCREEN
Click the REVENGE button — the red "STAND DOWN" alert fires. Then click FLOW to reset. Click LOG BASELINE & ENTER MARKET.
02
MODULE 02 — Habit Guardrails
0:22 – 0:38
SCREEN
On the GUARDRAILS tab. Quickly check off each item in the Entry Criteria checklist — watch the green glow animate in on each row. Show the Readiness Score ticking up: 0/9... 3/9... 7/9...
VOICE
"Then you run the ICT checklist. Order block confirmed. Liquidity swept. Wick valid. Candle closed. Not until all nine boxes are green do you have permission to enter. That discipline streak at the bottom? That's your real P&L."
SCREEN
Check remaining boxes — Readiness Score hits 9 / 9 and turns green: "Fully locked. ENTER THE MARKET." Scroll down briefly to show the 28-day streak grid.
TIP
Slow down slightly on the 9/9 moment — it's satisfying and the viewer needs to register what just happened.

MODULE 03 — Post-Market Review Loop
0:38 – 0:50
SCREEN
Navigate to REVIEW LOOP. Quickly type a few words into the "What was my plan?" and "What did I actually do?" fields — doesn't need to be long. Click the ELITE (9) self-score button.
VOICE
"After the session, you close the loop. What did you plan? What did you actually do? What's the gap? Steenbarger's performance loop — Plan, Act, Review — built directly into the flow. Your edge compounds every time you fill this out."
04
MODULE 04 — AI Sentiment Engine
0:50 – 1:04
SCREEN
Navigate to SENTIMENT. The 4 market cards (NQ, Gold, Oil, BTC) are already showing scores. Click REFRESH AI SENTIMENT SCAN — the cards fade to 30% opacity, the spinner appears, then the new AI-generated scores and catalysts animate back in.
VOICE
"One tap. Live AI sentiment across NASDAQ, Gold, Oil, and Bitcoin. Sentiment scores, the key catalysts driving each market, and how they're correlating right now. You walk into the open with context — not guesses."
TIP
Do the refresh before recording so you know it works. The live API call is real — have a backup static screenshot ready in case of latency.
05
MODULE 05 — AI Chart Analyzer
1:04 – 1:18
SCREEN
Navigate to CHART AI. Have a chart screenshot pre-saved on your desktop. Click the upload zone, select the file — the preview appears immediately. Click ANALYZE WITH AI. The spinner runs briefly, then Claude's structured analysis populates: trend, OBs, liquidity zones, outlook.
VOICE
"Screenshot any chart. NQ, Gold, BTC — doesn't matter. AI reads the price action using ICT and SMC methodology. Order blocks, fair value gaps, liquidity sweeps, technical outlook. In seconds."
TIP
Use a clean, high-contrast chart screenshot for this demo — ideally NQ on a 15-min with clear OBs visible. The more structured the chart, the more impressive the AI output looks.

Recording Setup
Open the HTML file locally in Chrome at 1920×1080. Use Loom or OBS at 60fps. Record voiceover separately for cleaner audio if possible.
Pacing Note
Move deliberately — don't rush clicks. Pause half a second after each interaction to let animations complete before speaking the next line.
Music Suggestion
Dark cinematic electronic, 80–90 BPM. Avoid anything with lyrics. YouTube Audio Library: search "cinematic dark electronic" or "tech noir ambient."
Thumbnail Frame
